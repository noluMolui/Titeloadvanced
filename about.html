About Us

We are a leading provider of innovative electrical solutions for residential, commercial, and industrial clients across Gauteng Province. Founded by Buti Mokwana, our mission is to transform the electrical services industry with smart, eco-friendly solutions that enhance quality of life and promote environmental stewardship.

Since 2019, Titelo Electrical Projects has built a reputation for reliability, professionalism, and exceptional service. Our licensed electricians deliver safe, efficient, and cost-effective electrical solutions tailored to each client’s unique needs.

Our Vision

To become South Africa’s leading electrical projects company, delivering high-quality services and competitive solutions while promoting sustainability.

Our Mission

To provide innovative, reliable, and environmentally conscious electrical services that enhance customer satisfaction and community well-being.
